01 — The Developer

Hi, I'm JellyCode.
I make horror games.

My focus is horror — the kind that creeps up slowly and stays with you. I care about atmosphere, storytelling, and making you feel like something is just slightly wrong.

Puppet School: Underground is my latest chapter — built solo in Godot 4, with everything from the monster AI to the lore designed by me.

02 — The Game

Puppet School: Underground

Chapter 4 takes you deeper than before. Beneath Puppet School lie tunnels — old, experimental, forgotten. They were used to move things that couldn't be seen above ground.

Steven Jones descends into the underground looking for answers. What he finds is Andrew Oswald — the PuppetMaster — still pulling strings.

Navigate the tunnels. Avoid the monster. Find the truth. And maybe, if you're lucky, make it out.

03 — Key Features

What Waits Below

[HUNT]

Monster AI

The PuppetMaster doesn't wander randomly. It searches, chases, and listens. Make a sound and it will find you.

[MAP]

Underground Tunnels

A winding network of experimental corridors, transport shafts, and sealed rooms. Every path could be a trap.

[LORE]

Deep Storyline

Uncover the full history of Puppet School — Andrew Oswald, Felix Rayn, Bella Blue, and the experiments that started it all.

[END]

A True Finale

Chapter 4 builds to a confrontation years in the making. What happens to Steven Jones is up to you to discover.

[PIX]

Pixel Horror Aesthetic

Rendered in Blender with a pixel art pipeline. Every frame is intentional — dark, gritty, and unsettling.
